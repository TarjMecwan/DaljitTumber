What's Special

A classic Ranch on a
quiet San Ramon cul-de-sac.

Set at the end of a peaceful cul-de-sac in the sought-after Alamo Creek subdivision, this single-story Ranch-style home offers 4 bedrooms, 3 bathrooms, and 1,979 sqft of comfortable living space — all on a generous 6,969 sqft lot.

The home features hardwood, tile, and carpet flooring throughout. The kitchen is equipped with stone counters, a breakfast bar, a breakfast nook, gas range, dishwasher, and microwave — ideal for everyday cooking and casual dining. The family room includes a cozy fireplace, perfect for gathering.

A detached garage with 2 spaces and 6 total parking spots offers ample room for vehicles and storage, complemented by an on-site shed. The fenced backyard has plenty of space for gardening or play, and pool features are available — see remarks for details.

Located in San Ramon with easy access to San Ramon Valley's top-rated schools, parks, shopping, and major commute corridors. No HOA. Offered as-is — a great opportunity in one of the East Bay's most desirable communities.
