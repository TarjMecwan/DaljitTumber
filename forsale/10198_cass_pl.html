What's Special

A move-in ready retreat
on a quiet Cupertino street.

Tucked away on a no-through-traffic street in a highly desirable Cupertino neighborhood, this updated single-level home greets you with a tiled front porch and a classic foyer opening to formal living and dining rooms set across from each other.

Large windows fill the home with natural light. The elegant formal living room is especially spacious, anchored by a fireplace and designed for both entertaining and everyday comfort. The kitchen connects easily to a sunny breakfast nook with serene backyard views, and a separate family room offers a second fireplace and direct outdoor access.

Out back, enjoy a private, low-maintenance yard with patio space and fruit trees. The garage is finished with epoxy flooring, built-in storage cabinets, and an EV charger.

Just a short walk to Blackberry Farm Golf Course and the Stevens Creek Trail, and close to Stevens Creek Elementary, John F. Kennedy Middle, and Monta Vista High — plus Cupertino's Main Street, Highway 280, and the Apple campus.
